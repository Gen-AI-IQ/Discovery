# VMD GenIQ - Domain & Launch Strategy

---

## 1. Primary Recommendation: `vmaxaitech.com`

* **Brand Logic:** "VMax" keeps the spirit of the legal Udyam registration (Value Maximizer). "AI Tech" clearly tells new B2B clients and recruiters what skills you augment (GenAI-Augmented Product & Delivery Management) without restricting you to one division.
* **Pillar Versatility:** Covers staffing (providing AI Tech talent), consulting (architecting AI Tech), and SaaS (building AI Tech products).
* **SEO & AI Sourcing:** Maximum keyword relevance for AI-driven search crawlers (Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity) seeking AI-augmented services.
* **Email & Reach:** Passes the "Radio Test" (easy to pronounce and spell), is short (11 characters), and avoids spam filter flags.

---

## 2. Low-Cost Registrar Breakdown

To maintain a lean startup model, register the domains using flat-rate registrars that do not hike renewal fees.

### A. Primary Domain: `vmaxaitech.com`
* **Registrar:** **Spaceship.com**
  * **First-Year Promo:** **$5.67** (Apply coupon code **`COM67`** at checkout).
  * **Annual Renewal:** **$9.66/year** (flat rate, no surprise hikes).
  * **Privacy:** Free WHOIS protection forever.
* **At-Cost Alternative:** **Cloudflare Registrar**
  * **Registration & Renewal:** **~$9.77/year** (at-cost wholesale pricing).

### B. Regional Anchor: `vmaxaitech.in`
* **Registrar:** **Spaceship.com** (or BigRock / GoDaddy India).
  * **Registration & Renewal:** **~$4.50 - $5.50/year** (Priced locally to match India Udyam legal setup).

## 3. Deployment & Setup Checklist

1. **Procure Domain:** Purchase `vmaxaitech.com` and `vmaxaitech.in` on Spaceship.com.
2. **Point DNS to Cloudflare:** Point nameservers to Cloudflare to enable free SSL/HTTPS encryption.
3. **Onboard Professional Email:**
   * Register domain with **Zoho Mail's Forever Free plan** (`founder@vmaxaitech.com`).
   * Add **SPF**, **DKIM**, and **DMARC** authentication records in Cloudflare to guarantee email deliverability to recruiter ATS systems.
4. **Deploy Repository:** Link the Next.js project on GitHub to Vercel/Cloudflare Pages for continuous deployment.
